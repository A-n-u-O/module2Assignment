TYPES OF ROCKS

IGNEOUS ROCKS

formed from melted rock deep inside the earth. For example: gabbro, dacite, basalt, granite, obsidian, pegmatite e.t.c.

SEDIMENTARY ROCKS

formed from layers of sand,silt,dead plants and animal skeleton. e.g, chalk, shale, sandstone, banded iron e.t.c.

METAMORPHIC ROCKS

formed from other rocks that are changed by heat and pressure undergroung. e.g. jadeite, gneiss, marble, slate, schist,eclogite
